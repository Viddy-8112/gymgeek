## What to look for in a protein supplement

Before getting into specific products, here's what separates a good protein supplement from a mediocre one:

- **Protein per serving** — aim for at least 20–25g per serving
- **Protein percentage** — divide protein grams by total serving size. Anything above 70% is solid
- **Ingredient list** — fewer fillers and artificial ingredients is better
- **Price per gram of protein** — the most honest way to compare value across brands
